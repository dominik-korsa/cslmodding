About

Recently I got my first job as a 3D artist, moved to another country, and transitioned from 3ds Max to Maya.

I am still interested in continuing making content for Cities: Skylines, while also practicing Maya and making money, hence why this page exists.

Assets

I prefer simple non-functional assets - such as props, regular buildings, decorative networks, etc. - because they require less work in the asset editor. But still I may be willing to do more complicated assets - such as vehicles, animated buildings, networks, train stations - depending on how complex they are and how much it pays.

In the interest of practicing Maya and making money, I am more open to different styles of assets I usually wouldn't do.

Payment

In the past I have made content for anywhere from 15 - 120 EUR. I am highly unlikely to do anything under 20 EUR, maybe only if it's a very basic prop, decorative wall network or some small decal pack. Regular buildings or props could be about 30 - 50 EUR, while more complicated buildings or vehicles could be around 50 - 100 EUR. It all depends on how much I am interested in the style of the asset, the artistic complexity of the asset, and the amount of work required in the asset editor.

You can pay using PayPal or even a bank transfer to my Latvian bank account. Legally I am registered as a self-employed microcompany tax payer in Latvia.

Publishing

Depending on the complexity of the asset and how busy I am, it may take anywhere from a couple days to multiple months. In some cases I may start work on the asset and show my progress before the payment, but either way the asset will only be published after I have received the payment.

The final asset would be public on the steam workshop, but you may request to keep it private for some time, like some youtubers have done for upcoming series.
